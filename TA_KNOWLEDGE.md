## SMC/ICT Concepts
- Order Block: last opposing candle before impulsive move
- FVG (Fair Value Gap): 3-candle imbalance
- Liquidity Sweep: price wicks beyond swing high/low → reverses
- Entry: wait for sweep → MSS → retrace to OB/FVG
